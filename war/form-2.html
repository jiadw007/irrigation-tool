Step 2 - Select Soil Characteristics

Root depth refers to the depth of soil in which roots are found. In some places this may be limited by the amount of soil and in other places this may be limited by other factors.

The irrigation area represents the area that receives irrigation. If the value is below 1, you must include the 0 before the decimal point. Example, 0.5 instead of .5.
